Option 01 Full Horizontal Lockup
The Primary Lockup

The complete horizontal identity. The icon assembles ESi's full story in a single mark: a South Florida palm tree and crimson skyline establish the geographic context, a hexagonal inspection clipboard signals certification authority, and an engineering gear anchors the technical foundation. The large ESi wordmark to the right delivers immediate name recognition. This is the primary logo for all formal applications — website header, proposals, letterhead, and any context where the full system can be shown.

Option 02 Standalone Mark
The Icon Mark

The standalone mark — the icon system isolated from the wordmark. This version has a distinct compositional arrangement from the icon embedded in Option 01. The buildings are more vertically prominent and given greater visual weight as the dominant element, the palm tree stands more independently at left, and the overall mark is balanced for square-format use rather than compressed to fit within a horizontal lockup. Every element — the skyline, palm, hexagonal inspection clipboard, engineering gear, and water sweep — is present and calibrated for independent legibility at reduced sizes. Use for app icons, social media avatars, embossed letterhead badges, embroidery patches, and any context requiring a self-sufficient standalone symbol.

Option 03 Architectural Frame
The Contained Mark

A structural rectangular frame — reading as a building elevation with walls and a defined roofline — holds the ESi lettermark at its base while a crimson skyline breaks free above. The simplest and most architectural of the five options. The diagonal-cut S in the lettermark preserves typographic continuity across the identity system. Strong as a profile image, document seal, app icon variant, or any square-format application.

Option 04 Engineering Authority
The Authority Mark

A single-color mark built entirely in deep crimson. The city skyline rises from the ESi lettermark frame, a structural gear references engineering precision on the left, and a shield with a certification checkmark sits at center — the most direct visual reference to ESi's core purpose: inspection and official approval. This variation demonstrates how the full identity system performs when reduced to one color, preserving every element of the mark's meaning without requiring a second ink or hue.

Option 05 Structural Column
The Precision Lockup

The most restrained and modern of the five. A compact building icon — crimson facade panels beside a structural column with horizontal rebar detail — sits alongside the large ESi wordmark in midnight navy. The rebar element is a deliberate, expert-level reference to structural engineering that reads clearly to industry professionals without being heavy-handed for general audiences. The cleanest and most scalable option in the system, ideal for digital interfaces, signage, and contexts where visual clarity at small sizes is critical.

Option 06 Logotype
Exclusive Typographic Treatment

A dedicated logotype with typography designed exclusively for this identity system. The ESi lettermark is integrated directly into the wordmark — the E and S in oversized weight serve as the visual anchor while "Engineering Services, inc." runs alongside in a custom condensed structure. No separate icon mark is required. The logotype functions as a complete, self-contained typographic identity when a purely typographic expression of the brand is preferred.
